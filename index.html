Mission

Bitcoin offers an inflation-resistant digital store of value. A true digital money. Our mission is to educate about the benefits of adopting a Bitcoin Standard and to promote safe and responsible bitcoin adoption.

Organization

We hope to contribute to building a community of bitcoin enthusiasts in Denmark. By organizing meet-ups, talks and workshops we hope to spread bitcoin knowledge and to learn from each other.

About

We are a group of people who are passionate about sound money. We believe everyone should have the ability to save for the future, in a currency that is resistant to inflation. We believe bitcoin will enable this and we meet regularly to share, teach and learn how. We are open to everyone who wants to join us.
